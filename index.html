Questions

Before you reach out

What do you need from me to get started?

Raw footage, any brand assets you have (logos, fonts, colours), two or three reference videos you like, and a short brief on who the video is for. That's usually enough for me to start on the first cut.

What's your turnaround time?

It depends on length and complexity, so we agree a delivery date before any work starts. Shorts move fastest; long-form documentary-style edits take longer. Gold and Platinum tiers get priority in my queue.

How many revisions are included?

Silver includes two revision rounds. Gold and Platinum include more generous revision allowances — we'll confirm the exact terms in your quote before we begin.

How do you deliver the final files?

You get upload-ready exports via Google Drive, Dropbox or Frame.io — whichever you prefer. Thumbnails and packaging assets come in the same delivery. Project files are available on request.

Do you take on monthly retainers?

Yes. Most of my best work comes from ongoing relationships where I learn a channel's voice over time. Reach out and we'll structure a monthly arrangement around your publishing cadence.

How does payment work?

I run contracts through Upwork, which protects both sides with escrow and a clear record of scope. If you'd rather work directly, message me on WhatsApp and we'll sort out something that works for you.
